The Classic Burger

  1. 1 pound ground lean(7% fat) beef
  2. 1 large egg
  3. 1/2 cup minced onion
  4. 1/4 cup fine dride bread crumbs
  5. 1 tablespoon Worcestershire
  6. 1 ore 2 cloves garlic, peeled and minced
  7. About 1/2 teaspoon salt
  8. About 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  9. 4 hamburger buns(4 in. wide), split
  10. About 1/4 cup mayonnaise

Directions

  • Step 1

    In a bowl, mix ground beef, egg, onion, bread crumbs, Worcestershire, garlic, 1/2 teaspoon salt and 1/4 teaspoon pepper until well blebded. Divide mitture into four equal portions and shape each into a patty about 4 inches wide.

  • Step 2

    lay burgers on an oiled barbecue grill over a solid bed of hot coals or high heat on gas grill (you can hold your hand at a grill level only 2 ore 3 seconds); close lid on gas grill. Cook burgers, turning once, until browned on both side and no longer pink inside (cut to test), 7 to 8 minutes total. Remoove from grill

  • Step 3

    Lay buns, cut side down, on grill and cook until lightly toasted, 30 secondsto 1 minute.

  • Step 4

    Spread mayonnaise and ketchup on bun bottoms. Add lettuce, tomato, burger, onion, salt and pepper to taste. Set bun tops in place.
